Guessing Game

There will be a number sequence of three numbers randomly generated. The lowest the numbers will reach is 0 and the highest is 9. Each box will hold a number.
The player will enter a number into the boxes to guess the number inisde of the box. If the guess is correct the box will turn green.
If the guess is in the current sequence, but in a different box the box will turn yellow. If the guess is incorrect the box will turn red.
There will be a total of 10 tries allowed. The game can be restarted once completed or failed.
